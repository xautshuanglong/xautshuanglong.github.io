### 常用命令：

| hexo 命令|范例|说明|
|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|
|new "title"|hexo new "title"|创建一篇新文章|
|new draft "title"|hexo new draft "title"|创建一篇新草稿|
|publish [layout] "title"|hexo publish post "title"|发布一篇草稿 layout=post\page|
